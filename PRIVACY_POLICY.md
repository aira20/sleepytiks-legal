# Sleepy Tiks Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

Sleepy Tiks may collect and process:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server IDs
* Discord Channel IDs
* Ticket information
* Transaction details submitted by users
* Messages submitted through ticket forms
* Audit and moderation logs

We do not collect passwords, payment card information, or Discord login credentials.

## 2. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

* Create and manage tickets.
* Facilitate transaction workflows.
* Provide support services.
* Maintain security and moderation records.
* Improve Bot functionality.

## 3. Data Storage

Information may be stored in databases used by Sleepy Tiks for operational purposes.

Data may be retained as long as necessary to provide services, maintain records, or comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only:

* When required by law.
* To investigate fraud or abuse.
* To maintain service security.
* With authorized server administrators where necessary for ticket management.

## 5. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Users should avoid submitting highly sensitive personal information through tickets.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Sleepy Tiks operates on Discord and may rely on third-party hosting, database, analytics, or infrastructure providers.

These providers may process information necessary for operation of the service.

## 7. User Rights

Users may request information regarding data associated with their account, subject to operational and legal limitations.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Sleepy Tiks is not intended for individuals who are prohibited from using Discord under Discord's Terms of Service.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of any revisions.

## 10. Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to the Sleepy Tiks administration team through the official Discord server.
